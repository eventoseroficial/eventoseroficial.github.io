Submission Rules

Talks Videos Submission Rules

We are receiving proposals for lectures until 3/20/2022, free theme, using R.

To do this, it is necessary to send a summary and/or a video call of no more than 2 minutes in an mp4 file.

The answer for including your lecture in the event schedule will occur until 4/3/2022 and, you will have until 5/7/2022 to send the final file of your video.

The lecture must have a maximum of 20 minutes in mp4 file.

Here there are suggestions and tips on how to record the video.

The name and photo from your Goolgle account will be register when you upload your files to this form.
